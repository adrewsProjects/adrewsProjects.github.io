Welcome!

Thanks for reading more about the activity! If you're interested in participating here's what happens:

  • You'll be seated at a table and asked to learn about a piece of lab equipment. You'll have to use your phone to access a website.
  • You'll fill out a paper survey and answer some questions.
  • When you turn in the survey you'll receive a $5 gift card to Starbucks.
  • It should take about 10-20 min to complete the study.

Requirements for participation

  • You must be 18 years of age or older.
  • You must be a currently enrolled engineering student (any level) at UC San Diego.
  • You cannot be enrolled in or have completed CENG 170 (it's ok if you plan to complete that course in the future).
  • You have not participated in this study previously.
  • You have a phone and can use it to access websites.
  • You have to review the consent form (below).

Data collection

No identifiable information is retained as part of the study (no names, no photos, no computer logs, etc.). Your Student PID is collected only to prevent people from repeating the study. It's not linked to your answers, it's not used to access any of your student information in any way at any time, it's stored in encrypted form only, and the record is destroyed as soon as all gift cards have been given out.
