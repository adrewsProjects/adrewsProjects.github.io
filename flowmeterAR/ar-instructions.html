AR Tool Description

This page describes how to use the augmented reality (AR) tool (app) for this study. Please review these brief instructions then click the link at the bottom of this page to go to the app.

Allowing access

The app uses your camera and motion sensors to deliver an AR experience. You'll need to click ok a few times to allow access to these resources. No recordings are made or stored. The Chrome browser cannot be used; other browsers like Safari and Firefox should be fine.

Using the app

The object on the test stand in front of you has a target marker on it that looks like this:

While using the app, hold your camera about an arm's length from the target marker. Slowly move your camera around until the animated overlay appears.

Tips

  • The target marker must always be visible.
  • Try not to point the camera directly at the marker (this can cause shaking).
  • Keep your phone upright (portrait) not horizontal (landscape).
  • Move your phone slowly as you explore the scene.
  • Sliders adjust transparency of some elements.

Accessing the app

When you're ready, click the button below to go to the app. Remember: it will ask for permission to access your camera and motion devices.
