Background

You are being invited to participate in a research study titled "Web-based augmented reality tool (ART) for Engineering Laboratory Applications". This study is being done by Prof. Aaron Drews from UC San Diego. You were selected to participate in this study because you volunteered to participate as a UC San Diego engineering student.

Purpose of study

The purpose of this research study is evaluate various methods of short-term instruction for lab devices, like written instructions, instructional videos, and augmented reality tools. Your participation in this research should last approximately 20 minutes. If you agree to take part in this study, you will be asked to read instructions, watch a video, or use an augmented reality tool to learn about a lab device, then complete a survey about your experiences and what you learned.

Participation is voluntary

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary and you can withdraw at any time. Choosing not to participate or withdrawing will result in no penalty or loss of benefits to which you are entitled. You are free to skip any question that you choose.
